Cupcake

Description

Easy and fast to cook

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Wheat flour
  • Milk
  • Melted butter
  • Baking powder

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, pour one ingredient at a time and mix everything together until an even mixture is created. Put it in molds and put it in the oven, at medium temperature, for 30 minutes. While the batter is roasting, melt the chopped chocolate in a double boiler or in the microwave (be careful not to burn!) and mix with the table cream. Then refrigerate until it hardens, reaching an ointment point. To spread the cover over the cupcakes, use a spout or spoon. Add chocolate sprinkles to taste.
