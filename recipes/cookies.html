Cookies

Description

Delicious and simpliest recipe of cookies

Ingredients

  • Margarine
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Egg
  • Wheat flour
  • Baking powder
  • Chopped chocolate
  • Cinnamon and vanilla

Instructions

  1. InglÃªs As with making cupcakes, you will need a large bowl. In it, add the margarine, sugar and brown sugar and beat until you get a uniform mixture. Add the egg and continue mixing. While still stirring, add the wheat flour, cinnamon and vanilla. Finally, add the baking powder. Then, with the batter already prepared, make small balls, using your hands. Place them in a greased mold (to prevent them from sticking) and flatten each of the balls made earlier, making sure you leave space between them. Place the pan in a preheated oven at 200 degrees Celsius for about 20 minutes or until the edges of the cookies are darker. Remove from oven, unmold after cooling and delight in the kids!
