Hot Dog

Description

A quickly hot-dog recipe

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • Sausages
  • Tomato paste or tomato sauce–500g
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Hot dog bread

Instructions

  1. Pour the chopped onion after heating the required amount of oil in a large pan and sauté until golden. Add tomato paste (or tomato sauce), salt and heat for 5 minutes. After that, add the sausages and cook for another 5 minutes. Place a sausage on each loaf and add sauce and extra ingredients to taste. Afterwards, just taste your hot dog with the kids!
